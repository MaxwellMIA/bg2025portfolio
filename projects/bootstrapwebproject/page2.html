Toons

Toons are a huge part of the game's mechanics, as every toon has different abilities and different stats. There are different types of toons, Extractor, Distractor, Survivalist, Supporter, and Healers, and they help keep the game rolling smoothly.

Extractor

Extractors do the machines, trying to stay out of danger. Extractors are the fastest toons at extracting, either with having a high skillcheck, or a fast extracting speed.

Distractor

Distractors help distract the twisted by having the twisteds constantly following them. The distractors have high speed and sometimes high stamina too.

Survivalist

Supporter

Supporters help support the team, either by giving them boosts or by being a small extractor or distractor.

All-Rounder

Stats allow you to do many roles and be able to switch mid-game if needed. Very helpful if your main distractor or extractor dies.

Healer

Healers heal themselves or only other players. Being the healer is a hard job but one of the most useful.

Toons also have stats, which make them useful in different areas and assigns them to a role. You can always look at a Toons stats to know what type of role your playing.

  • Health

    Health is how many hearts you have, if you lose them all its game over.

  • Skill check

    Skillcheck is how big your skillcheck is, the bigger it is, the easier it is to hit the stillcheck.

  • Movement Speed

    Movement speed is how fast you walk and run, the higher it is, the faster you are.

  • Stamina

    Stamina is how long you can run without needed to stop. The longer your stamina the longer you can run.

  • Stealth

    Stealth is how long your range of being seen by twisteds. The higher your stealth, you closer you can be to twisteds without them seeing you

  • Extraction Speed

    Extraction speed is how fast you do machines, the faster the better.
