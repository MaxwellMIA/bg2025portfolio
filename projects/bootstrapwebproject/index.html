Dandy's world

Dandy's world is a multiplayer mascot horror game on Roblox, you can play alone, or up to 8 people. Dandy's world is a free game, and is on computers, laptops, phones, and consoles. In Dandy's world you try to get to high floors by doing machines to advance, but be careful as you also try to avoid the twisteds, as they want to hurt you.
