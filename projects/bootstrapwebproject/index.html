Dandy's world

Dandy's world is a multiplayer mascot horror game in on Roblox that is in alpha, you can play alone, or up to 8 people. Dandy's world is a free game, and is on computers, laptops, phones, and consoles. In Dandy's world you try to get to high floors by doing machines to advance, but be careful as you also try to avoid the twisteds, as they want to hurt you.

Because the game is in alpha, There are some bugs , but also the map might change for events they are testing out. This was made during the chirstmas event, so everything is christmas themed. When the event is over, the images will be updated.
