  • Meet the team

There is a huge team behind Dandy’s world, but the most important face is Qwel. Qwel is the one who made the idea of Dandy’s world, and she was the one that got it running up. Qwels account is here, but that's not all behind Dandy’s world. Dandy’s world also has a group on Roblox for its players, and more importantly a Discord server. The Discord server is used for members to see upcoming updates, and for players to report bugs and hackers.

Qwel and her team do a great job of updating the game and making sure there are no toxic players or anyone cheating their way through the game. Without Qwel this game wouldn’t exist for its player base to enjoy.
